# Who Comes Aboard

Solo? Partner? Kids? Friends? Dog? This colors everything — safety content for a family with kids, social spots for entertainers, solo tips for single-handers.

## How to learn this
Don't ask directly. It comes out naturally: "bringing the family this weekend?" or they'll mention crew when talking about trips.
