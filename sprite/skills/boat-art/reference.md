# Boat Art — Style Catalog

## Clean / Illustration
- `clean-line` — Precise ink outlines, flat color fills, vector-like
- `colored-pencil` — Warm pencil strokes on white paper
- `blueprint` — White lines on deep blue, engineering schematic

## Painterly
- `watercolor` — Transparent washes, wet-on-wet bleeds
- `japanese-woodblock` — Ukiyo-e style, bold outlines, Hokusai-inspired

## Bold / Graphic
- `pop-art` — Ben-Day dots, Lichtenstein-inspired
- `comic-book` — Bold ink outlines, halftone shading
- `retro-poster` — 1950s travel poster, flat shapes
- `neon-glow` — Glowing neon outlines on dark background
- `stencil` — Banksy-inspired, high contrast spray paint

## Screen Print / Production
- `screen-print` — 2-3 color separation, vintage tee aesthetic
- `risograph` — Overlapping color layers, grainy indie poster

## Nautical / Maritime
- `nautical-tattoo` — Traditional sailor tattoo, bold old-school
- `nautical-engraving` — Copper plate etching, fine crosshatch
- `vintage-chart` — Aged nautical chart, sepia parchment

## Coastal / Lifestyle
- `tiki` — Tiki bar poster, tropical retro polynesian
- `vintage-florida` — 1960s Florida postcard, sun-bleached pastels
- `surf-art` — 1970s surf culture, earthy flat colors

## Craft / Handmade
- `embroidery` — Embroidered patch look, visible thread texture
- `paper-cutout` — Layered paper cut art, dimensional depth

## Modern / Digital
- `flat-vector` — Clean minimal vector, app-icon crisp
- `cartoon` — Bright and playful, exaggerated proportions
- `pixel-art` — 16-bit retro game aesthetic

## Monochrome
- `mono-navy` — Single navy blue on white, timeless maritime
- `chalk` — White chalk on dark chalkboard
- `sepia-sketch` — Warm brown tones on cream

## Premium / Specialty
- `art-deco` — Gold and navy, 1920s Gatsby glamour
- `linocut` — Bold carved lines, black/white with one accent
- `gold-foil` — Metallic gold line art on dark navy
- `psychedelic` — 1960s concert poster, swirling vibrant colors
