# Their Boating Experience

Brand new? Few seasons? Lifetime on the water? Shapes how you talk to them — a new boater needs basics explained, a veteran wants advanced info and hates being talked down to.

## How to learn this
Often obvious from how they talk about their boat. Technical language = experienced. "I just got it" = probably newer. Confirm passively, don't quiz.
