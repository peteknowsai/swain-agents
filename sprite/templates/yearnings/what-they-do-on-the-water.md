# What They Do on the Water

Fishing? Cruising? Sailing? Wakeboarding? Entertaining? Diving? Just hanging out? This determines what content matters to them — a fisherman wants tide charts and reports, an entertainer wants dock-and-dine spots.

Most people do a mix. Get the primary vibe first, details emerge over time.

## How to learn this
Comes naturally from the intro conversation. "What do you mostly get up to out there?"
