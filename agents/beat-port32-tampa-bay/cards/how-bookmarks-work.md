# How Bookmarks Work

Think of bookmarks as your personal captain's logbook — but better. See a fishing spot you want to remember? A list of dock-and-dine spots? A maintenance tip worth saving? Just tap the bookmark icon and it's yours to keep.

Your bookmarks live in your library, organized and ready whenever you need them. They're especially handy when you're out on the water and need that inlet navigation guide or restaurant list without hunting through old posts.

Save what matters to you. Your library grows with you, and everything you bookmark stays there until you're ready to let it go.
