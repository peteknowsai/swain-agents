## Local Knowledge
- Bunces Pass: Main artery to Gulf, strong currents on spring tides, bar ~4ft at low
- Egmont Channel: Deep shipping channel, current runs hard on big swings
- Shell Key Flats: Get stranded on falling tide = stuck for hours
- Current runs 1-2 hours after tide turns at Egmont; Bunces peaks mid-tide
- Key data: Egmont Key Station 8726347, TPA0401 current predictions
