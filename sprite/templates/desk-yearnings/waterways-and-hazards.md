# Waterways & Hazards

What are the major channels, passes, inlets, bridges? Any shoaling areas, tricky currents, restricted zones? Where do people anchor? What are the main routes?

## How to learn this
Research local cruising guides, NOAA charts, active notices to mariners for the area.
