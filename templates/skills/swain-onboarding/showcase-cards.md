# Onboarding Showcase Cards

Sample cards to send via WhatsApp during onboarding conversations. Send as image + caption text.

## Card 1: Dock-and-Dine (lifestyle/dining)
- **Image**: https://imagedelivery.net/7NA-8FN5mTUANBxov63ekA/bc272180-b116-46b1-5c24-9c4e2ef4ab00/public
- **Title**: Dock-and-Dine: Naples Waterfront
- **Subtext**: Pull up, tie off, walk to your table — The Dock at Crayton Cove, Tin City favorites, and hidden gems on Naples Bay and the Gordon River
- **Best for**: Broad appeal, strong visual (golden sunset). Good default lead.

## Card 2: Destinations (exploration)
- **Image**: https://imagedelivery.net/7NA-8FN5mTUANBxov63ekA/7e3777ac-c5aa-47b5-2c36-978229d9a200/card
- **Title**: Naples by Boat: 8 Destinations You Can't Reach by Car
- **Subtext**: From Keewaydin's pristine beaches to the Everglades backcountry, discover the hidden waterways that make Naples a world-class boating destination
- **Best for**: Cruisers, explorers, anyone who loves discovering new spots.

## Card 3: Safety/Conditions (practical)
- **Image**: https://imagedelivery.net/7NA-8FN5mTUANBxov63ekA/f75e6012-3794-4a62-b868-f40f5926af00/public
- **Title**: Gale Warning Through Saturday
- **Subtext**: NW winds 25-35 kt, seas 4-9 ft, wind chills in single digits
- **Best for**: Showing practical value — "we actually keep you safe."

## Usage

- Lead with dock-and-dine (strongest visual) unless you know they're into something specific
- Pick 1-2 to send, don't dump all three
- Send as image with caption via WhatsApp using `media=<url>` on the message tool
- Weave into conversation naturally, e.g. "Here's a taste of what I send every day 👇"

## Note

These are Naples-specific samples. As we expand, we'll need showcase cards per location. For now these work for any onboarding since they demonstrate the content quality and style.
