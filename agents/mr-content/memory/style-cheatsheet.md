# Style Cheat Sheet — Mr. Content's Go-To Picks

When dispatching the stylist, pick from these based on card mood/content. Write a custom prompt that channels the style's vibe — don't just paste the promptText mechanically.

## Fishing & Species
- **Field Guide Plate** (`style_field-guide-plate`) — Audubon naturalist illustration. Perfect for species guides, fishing playbooks, bait guides.
- **Linocut Woodblock** (`style_linocut-woodblock`) — Bold carved print. Great for dramatic fish/species cards.
- **Copper Plate Etch** (`style_339baaf02009`) — Fine crosshatch engraving. Elegant species or technique cards.
- **Botanical Specimen** (`style_bb4f011a4105`) — Vintage scientific illustration. Bait guide, species ID.

## Destinations & Lifestyle
- **Golden Hour Glow** (`style_golden-hour-glow`) — Warm backlit silhouettes, sun flare. Sunset cruises, sandbar days.
- **1950s Travel Poster** (`style_1950s-travel-poster`) — Bold retro shapes. Island guides, "best trips" cards.
- **Kodachrome Summer** (`style_374ba89fe911`) — 70s film warmth. Weekend trips, lifestyle, nostalgia.
- **Aerial Drone** (`style_044b766b2662`) — Top-down. Sandbar guides, island overviews, anchorage maps.
- **Warm Watercolor** (`style_warm-watercolor`) — Soft painted feel. Destination guides, dock-and-dine.
- **Impressionist Harbor** (`style_7c9d9c6573d4`) — Monet brushwork. Waterfront dining, marinas, harbors.

## Safety & Navigation
- **Chalk on Blackboard** (`style_chalk-on-blackboard`) — Menu board feel. Safety checklists, how-to cards.
- **Blueprint Technical** (`style_082dcd8dd1e0`) — Engineering drawing. Navigation guides, channel guides.
- **Storm Drama** (`style_storm-drama`) — Dark dramatic weather. Storm safety, weather advisories.
- **Vintage Nautical Chart** (`style_vintage-nautical-chart`) — Old chart aesthetic. Navigation, inlet guides, tide cards.
- **Cross Section** (`style_5f22fd4cf0c4`) — Cutaway diagram. Engine care, anchoring mechanics.

## Maintenance & How-To
- **Knolling Layout** (`style_ba0c9dbc1864`) — Objects arranged overhead. Checklists, gear guides, pre-trip checks.
- **Gouache Flat** (`style_1ea37d0f96a7`) — Matte illustrated. How-to cards, explainer content.
- **Graphic Novel** (`style_19a553744018`) — Dark moody comic. Dramatic how-to (man overboard, engine failure).

## Port32 & Events
- **Isometric Marina** (`style_isometric-marina`) — SimCity diorama. Marina welcome guides, Port32 cards.
- **Art Deco Marina** (`style_b6969d04a232`) — Geometric luxury. Premium Port32 cards, upscale feel.
- **Toy Box 3D** (`style_8cd73ff1df3b`) — Playful 3D. Onboarding, friendly welcome cards.
- **Paper Cutout Collage** (`style_paper-cutout-collage`) — Craft style. Events, family content.

## Weather
- **Storm Drama** (`style_storm-drama`) — Already using. Advisories, warnings.
- **Misty Morning** (`style_misty-morning`) — Atmospheric fog. Calm conditions, morning reports.
- **Sunrise Gradient** (`style_061b10ca7848`) — Color gradient sky. Fair weather, positive outlooks.

## Fun / Variety
- **Nautical Tattoo Flash** (`style_nautical-tattoo-flash`) — Old school tattoo. Fun lifestyle cards, raft-up etiquette.
- **Neon Sign** (`style_neon-sign`) — Glowing neon. Nightlife, bar crawl cards, full moon cruises.
- **Diorama Box** (`style_ee47f5cbff58`) — Miniature scene. Unique standout cards.
- **Bioluminescent Bay** (`style_1ee217c7e319`) — Glowing water. Night fishing, full moon content.
- **Claymation Scene** (`style_ac90a24273fc`) — Stop-motion feel. Kids/family content.

## Rules
- **Vary styles within a category** — two fishing cards shouldn't get the same style back-to-back
- **Match mood to content** — a fun raft-up card ≠ a man overboard drill
- **Use the full range** — don't default to the same 5 styles
- **Style informs but doesn't dictate** — write a custom prompt that captures the card's unique scene PLUS the style's aesthetic
