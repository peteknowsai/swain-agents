# Where They Keep Their Boat

Marina wet slip? Dry stack? Trailer at home? Mooring? This shapes everything — their range, their routine, what conditions matter to them.

A trailer captain might boat anywhere in driving distance. A marina captain boats from one home base. Totally different content needs.

## How to learn this
Lead with it in the intro. People love talking about where their boat lives.
"Where do you keep her?"
