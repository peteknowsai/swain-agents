# Your Daily Briefing

Every morning, your Swain wakes up before you do.

While you're still reaching for coffee, they're already gathering intel: checking conditions at your favorite spots, scanning for events you'd actually want to know about, picking up local tips that matter to you. By the time you open the app, your Daily Briefing is waiting — a personalized morning update built just for you.

This isn't a generic forecast or a wall of news you'll never read. It's curated. Your Swain learns what you care about over time, what you skip, what makes you take action. The more you use it, the smarter it gets.

Think of it as your morning advantage. Everything you need to know, nothing you don't, delivered before your day gets away from you.
