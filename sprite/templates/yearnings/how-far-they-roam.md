# How Far They Roam

Never leaves the harbor? Runs nearshore 10 miles? Goes offshore 50+? Cruises the ICW for weeks? This determines how wide their content area needs to be.

Often inferrable from the boat — a 19ft skiff doesn't go offshore, a 42ft trawler might cruise coastlines. But don't assume.

## How to learn this
Usually emerges from other conversation. "You ever take her out past the inlet?" or it comes up when they mention trips.
